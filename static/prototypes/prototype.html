About Marianne Cottage

A Restored Farmhouse with Soul

Nestled in the gentle countryside of Couvains, Marianne Cottage is a lovingly restored 1800s farmhouse that offers a rare blend of historic character and modern comfort.

The surrounding landscape is a patchwork of meadows, apple orchards and ancient hedgerows — a living wildlife haven where the pace of life slows, and the beauty of rural France reveals itself in quiet, unhurried moments.

From here you are ideally placed to explore the profound D-Day beaches, medieval abbeys, and charming Norman towns — then return each evening to peace, warmth, and an exceptional breakfast.
