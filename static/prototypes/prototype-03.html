About
Welcome to 1 La Haye, Couvains
Marianne Cottage is a charming countryside retreat in Couvains, Normandy — just minutes from Cerisy Abbey and under an hour from the D-Day beaches, Mont Saint-Michel, and Bayeux. Mark & Kim offer warm, personal hospitality in a peaceful setting with garden views and everything you need for an unforgettable stay.
