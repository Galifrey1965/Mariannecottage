Accommodation

Your rooms

Marianne Cottage offers a private two-bedroom suite arrangement — ideal for families, couples travelling together, or a solo traveller seeking extra space. Both rooms share a beautifully appointed walk-in shower bathroom.

🛏
Bedroom One

The Double

A generous double-bedded room dressed in crisp linens, with garden views and soft natural light. An electric kettle and thoughtful touches make it a true retreat after a day of exploration.

🛏🛏
Bedroom Two

The Twins

Two comfortable single beds in a calm, well-appointed room — perfect for friends travelling together or a pair of younger guests. Garden-facing outlook and the same quality linens as the suite.

🚿
Shared Facilities

Modern Walk-in Shower Bathroom

A contemporary, generously proportioned wet-room style bathroom, exclusively for your suite. Powerful rain shower, quality towels, and premium toiletries provided.
